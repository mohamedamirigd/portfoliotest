Restaurant Branding

Complete brand identity for a modern restaurant.

Overview

Client: Local Restaurant

Role: Brand Designer

Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop

Challenge

The client needed a modern and memorable identity to stand out in a competitive market. Their previous branding lacked consistency and visual impact.

Solution

I created a strong visual identity including logo, typography, and color system. The design focuses on simplicity, elegance, and strong brand recognition.

Visual Output

Result

The new branding improved the restaurant’s visibility and created a strong and cohesive identity across all platforms.
